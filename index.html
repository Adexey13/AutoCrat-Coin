Overview

Notcoin is a decentralized digital currency that utilizes blockchain technology to enable secure and transparent transactions.

Features

  • Fast and low-cost transactions
  • Decentralized network
  • Immutable ledger
  • Secure and private

How It Works

Notcoin operates on a peer-to-peer network where transactions are verified by network nodes through cryptography and recorded on a public ledger called a blockchain.
